Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15 August 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Wherostr ("we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our decentralized social network app built on the Nostr protocol.

2. Information We Collect

Given the decentralized nature of Nostr, we collect minimal personal data. However, we may gather the following information:

  • Public Information: This includes any data you choose to broadcast publicly via the Nostr network, such as your profile information, posts, and interactions with others.
  • User Identifiers: Nostr utilizes public and private keys as user identifiers. Your public key may be used by others to follow your activity on the network.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect may be used in the following ways:

  • To Facilitate Network Operations: Your public information and user identifiers are used to ensure seamless interactions within the decentralized network.
  • To Improve User Experience: We may use anonymized data to analyze usage trends and improve app functionality.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Due to the decentralized nature of Nostr:

  • Public Data: Any information you broadcast on Nostr is inherently public and may be viewed by anyone with access to the network.
  • Third-Party Relays: Nostr relies on third-party relays to distribute data. While these relays may have their own privacy practices, they do not control the data.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your information. However, given the decentralized architecture, you are responsible for safeguarding your private keys. We strongly advise against sharing your private keys with anyone.

6. Your Rights

As a user of a decentralized network, you maintain control over your data. You can choose which information to broadcast and have the ability to delete or modify your data at any time.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted within the app and will take effect immediately.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@mapboss.co.th.
